Training day

Materials and agenda for the training of our new employees

Overview

The training day currently is indeed one day dedicated to introduction of how we do things at Sprettur when it comes to developing software. The plan for the day goes like this:

  1. Introduction to general agile software development
  2. TDD, types of tests and workflow
  3. Continuous Delivery
  4. Hands on exercise/pairing
